Teaching Practice

My Teaching Philosophy

I believe that all students are infinitely unique and infinitely interesting. They all have something special to offer and I see it as my role to help cultivate whatever this may be. I emphasise the relational aspect of teaching and getting to know the whole of the student, to ensure all students feel safe, respected, and connected to each other and their community. I also will exhibit high expectations and a strong work ethic in my role to imbed these traits in my students and inspire a growth mindset.
