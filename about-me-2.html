About Me

Professional Overview

I am currently a Learning Support Officer at Buckley Park College in Essendon. My role has been to develop and implement a numeracy support program that is aimed at improving the learning outcomes of students who are significantly behind the curriculum standards in maths. Each group has 2 half an hour sessions per week, where content runs in parallel to what is being covered in class. I structure the content so the beginning of a topic is usually a curriculum level or 2 below what is being covered in regular classes, and work our way up to that. For example: if students are covering VC2M9A01 in class, we will work thorugh the appropriate sequence of content descriptors (VC2M7A01, VC2M8A01) until we finally get towards that outcome. So far, this has been very successful according to the data captured, which shows significant growth in students grades, and this growth is significantly more then the average growth for students not participating in the program for the given year levels.
